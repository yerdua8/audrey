The Rubies: A history

"Music is very spiritual, it has the power to bring people together." - Edgar Winter

The Rubies started out as a song writing duet between the singer and lead guitarist, and developed into a full blown band once our bassist and drummer found the band. As edgar Winter said, music brought us together.
